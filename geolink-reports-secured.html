GeoLink Reports

Introduction

GeoLink Reports is the reporting platform for GeoLink (marketing DSP). We got complaints from the users (clients) about how hard it was to use GeoLink Reports. The PM was also on the same page and prioritize the problem for the quarter, so I was able to look into it.

The effort started with research, and ended up with a brand new redesign that improved the usability from above-average to excellent (SUS score from 72.5 to 91.25).

"What's' GeoLink Reports?"

Existing Platform

Introduction

GeoLink Reports is the reporting platform for GeoLink (marketing DSP for agencies and brands). It offers reporting for the campaigns, the users can access online dashboard and/or download reports to understand important metrics, including pacing, performance, CTR and so on.

Online Dashboard

The user flow goes: Start > Select Report Category > Select Report > Configure Report Filters > Load Dashboard > End

Report Download

The user flow goes: Start > Select Download > Select Campaign > Press "Download" Button > Save Report to Local > End

"What did you deal with?"

Problem & User

I met with the PM and came to the initial understanding:

Problem


GeoLink Clients, sales, and account managers complained that GeoLink Reports was hard to use. Why?

User


GeoLink clients. Was it all? Could it be more specific?

"How to discover more from the internal team?"

Internal Interview

Findings about Problem

Usability Related

  • Bad User Experience
  • Not Intuitive
  • Confusing
  • Difficult Navigation
  • Don’t Know What To Expect for Various Reports

Affordance Related

  • Too Many Unfamiliar Report Categories

Findings about User

External User

  • GeoLink Clients - Ad Ops (to review campaign performance)
  • GeoLink Clients - CMOs (to review campaign performance)

Internal User

  • Account Managers (to gather campaign performance reports for clients)

Key Decision

Design for both external and internal users?

No! I had a meeting with PM and account managers, we decided to design primarily for external clients, because they brought in revenue. But the design should also made sure to at least work for account managers.

"How to discover more from the users?"

User Interview

Findings about Problem

  • Usability and affordance were big issues.
  • The users were generally happy with the existing flow, and they didn’t want a totally different design.
  • Reports download could not apply date range, and it's a painpoint for the users.

Findings about User

  • CMO actually didn not use GeoLink Reports, our internal team was WRONG!
  • The users were all titled "Ad Ops".

"How to quantify the usability?"

Usability Survey

Usability Survey Findings

  • SUS Score = 72.5 (out of 100)
  • The score indicated that the usability of the system was slightly above average, and a long way from good usability.

"Any guidelines for design?"

Design Principles

Affordance

The new GeoLink Reports got a lot of complaints over users not knowing what report is what. The new design should apply affordance improvement.

Efficiency

The new GeoLink Reports should put efficiency at a high priority, because it’s a system for business users, and they cared about the speed to perform tasks.

Consistency

The new GeoLink Reports should reflect the new branding and design language. It could be achieved by using our InMarKit design system (Beta).
